Saraswathi Vidyalaya is an organisation which conducts Secondary and Higher Secondary Schools in Chennai, Kanchipuram and Nanganallur.


We are a progressive institution, interested in providing the best value to our students, parents, teachers and support staff.


Founded in 1956 by the late Smt. K Saraswathi, the school offers education from the playschool and kindergarten level up to class 12.
